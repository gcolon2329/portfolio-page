Whole Milk Studios
A Digital Production Collective

Whole Milk Studios

is a Miami based Digital Production Collective focused in creating digital content for media outlets big and small. Priding themselves in not compromising quality for budget. Working in a creative yet corporate industry, Whole Milk was looking for a simple yet creative branding that was going to stick out without being flashy. A company that lets their work speak for itself. Together we came up with Whole Milk Studios A Digital Production Collective.

BRANDING

Digital Media

Branching out from traditional advertising and tackling the new "millenial" ways, Whole Milk produces content for all social media platforms without excluding media outlets. Meaning the branding had to be clean yet versatile enough to play both sides.

Future Projects

Whole Milk App

With the changing industry and the younger generation paving the way for digital media, Whole Milk is always looking for ways to stay ahead of the trend. I began designing and building a social media app for Whole Milk in which content creators can not only upload and share their work but speak with and build crews for individualized projects.
